About ARS & Co.

ARS & Co is a distinguished firm of Company Secretaries committed to delivering high-quality professional services in Corporate Laws and allied regulatory matters. We provide comprehensive Legal, Secretarial, Financial, FEMA, Trademark, and Management Advisory Services to businesses ranging from owner-managed enterprises to large multinational corporations.

We specialize in advisory and compliance under The Companies Act, 2013; The Limited Liability Partnership Act, 2008; The Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999 (FEMA); Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016; and Other allied Corporate and Commercial Laws.

Our Associate

Mr. Nagalingam Muthaiah, Chartered Accountant

Resolution Professional registered with Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India, Registered Valuer in the Asset Class (Securities or Financial Assets), and certified Forensic Auditor.

Mr. Nagalingam possesses over 20 years of post-qualification experience in Statutory, System, Quality Standards, Management Audits, and Due Diligence for various Corporates, Firms, and Proprietorship entities.

He has provided advisory services on Financial, Statutory, and Compliance to various Corporates and did Statutory Audits of various Banks Branches. He has experience in preparation of Draft Prospectus for listing and Project Reports including Project financial management services. He is also specialized in Debt restructuring of MSME entities and Corporate entities including BIFR restructuring case under the SICA (Special Provisions) Act.

He has been part of CIRP process in NCLT, Chennai / Mumbai, and acted as Registered Valuer and Forensic Auditor for many Corporates.
